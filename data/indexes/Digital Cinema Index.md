# Digital Cinema Index

Topic index for Manovich's work on digital cinema — from his foundational 1995 thesis (cinema as a case of animation) through compositing analysis and hybrid moving images.

## Core Concepts

- [[Digital Cinema]] — Cinema redefined: live action as one input among many
- [[Compositing]] — Layering replacing montage as the dominant visual operation
- [[Velvet Revolution]] — The quiet spread of hybrid moving images (1993-98)
- [[Hybrid Media]] — New image species combining photography, animation, CGI, typography
- [[Automation of Vision]] — Machine seeing and synthetic realism
